# Quarterly / Trimester Tasks

The GSS fiscal year (June 1–May 31) is divided into **three trimesters**.
At the end of each trimester the FO has two formal reporting obligations to
Council.

---

## Trimester Financial Statements (Bylaw 8.5.6(b))

You must prepare and present a **complete budget and financial statements**
for the Society each trimester. These are distinct from the monthly financial
update — they provide a fuller picture of the Society's financial position at
each trimester boundary.

Work with the Accountant and GM to produce the trimester statements. The
Accountant is responsible for physical preparation; your role is to review,
verify, and present.

**Steps:**
1. Coordinate with the Accountant to obtain draft trimester statements.
2. Review for accuracy and notable variances.
3. Submit to HFC for review before Council presentation (within 5 weeks of
   trimester end per HF Policy 3.9(b)).
4. Present at the Council meeting following HFC review (no more than one
   month after HFC submission per HF Policy 3.9(c)).

---

Highlight any department where actuals are significantly ahead of or behind
budget. Flag any line items that may require a budget reallocation.

lines may be made by the FO in consultation with the Executives and the HFC.
Such redistributions must be reported to Council.
