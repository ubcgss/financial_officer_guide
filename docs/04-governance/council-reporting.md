# Council Reporting

The FO has two distinct Council reporting obligations: a monthly financial
update and trimester financial statements.

---

## Monthly Financial Report (Bylaw 8.5.6(a))

You are required to report to Council on the financial situation of the Society
on a monthly basis. Council meets on the **3rd Thursday of each month**,
except December where it is the **2nd Thursday**.

**Timeline for each monthly cycle:**

| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| Beginning of month (+3 business days) | Cardholders upload credit card expenses to Zoho Expenses |
| 1st working day of month | Department Heads approve credit card expenses in Zoho Expenses |
| 2nd working day of month | FO reviews and approves credit card expenses |
| Wednesday before Council week | Accountant delivers financial update report to FO |
| Monday of Council week (end of day) | FO reviews report and raises any questions |
| Thursday of Council week (Council meeting) | FO presents financial update to Council |
| Within 5 weeks of month end | FO and GM submit P&L and budget to HFC (HF Policy 3.9(b)) |

---

## Trimester Financial Statements (Bylaw 8.5.6(b))

Each trimester, you must prepare and present a complete budget and financial
statements for the Society. The three trimesters align with the fiscal year
(June 1–May 31); confirm the exact trimester boundaries with the GM and HFC.

**Steps:**
1. Coordinate with the Accountant to obtain draft trimester statements.
2. Review for accuracy, notable variances, and completeness.
3. Submit to HFC for review (within 5 weeks of trimester end, per HF
   Policy 3.9(b)).
4. Present to Council no more than one month after submitting to HFC (per
   HF Policy 3.9(c)).

---

## Council Agenda Item Format

When preparing your Council agenda item, use this structure:

```
Title:              [e.g., "Financial Officer Monthly Report — October 2024"]
Responsible party:  Financial Officer
Time:               [estimated minutes, typically 5–10]
Background:         [2–3 sentences summarizing the financial period:
                    revenues, major expenditures, notable variances]
Purpose/Objective:  To report to Council on the financial situation of the
                    Society pursuant to Bylaw 8.5.6(a).
Motion (if required):
  BIRT [motion text]
  — or —
  BIFRT [friendly amendment text]
```

For routine monthly reports, no motion is typically required unless Council
approval of a reallocation or unusual expenditure is needed.
